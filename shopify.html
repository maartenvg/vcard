My name is Maarten and I want to work with you

I'm a passionate software developer, currently living in Montréal. In July I'm moving to Ottawa, and I would love to work at shopify to continue doing what I do best: making great software.

Why do you want me?

You get someone experienced who gets things done and who can handle pressure. Someone who is comfortable with taking the lead, but also following it. And with my background and experience I might have a refreshing perspective on problems that you might have. I learn quickly and eagerly, so it never takes me long to adapt.

Background?

Originally I'm from the Netherlands, but moved to Canada in December. I studied Artificial Intelligence, and at the same time I was a self-employed PHP-developer. After university I started at Topicus, a SaaS company building complex web applications for health care in mostly Java and some C#.

There I moved very quickly from code slinger to lead developer of several long running projects. I was therefore involved in all parts of the product: design, development, testing, support and management.

What makes me happy?

Clean code, full range testing, secure software, open source software, tea and cats. Oh, and finally having my work permit is pretty darn awesome too.

Why shopify?

What and how you do things is exactly what I am looking for: working together with smart people, who understand how to produce great software, on an amazing product. There seems to be a good balance of hard work and fun, no suits and no 9-5. Plus, at Shopify I would get the opportunity to battle exciting new types of challenges, work with new technologies and never stop learning.

And Ruby?

Although these last few years were mostly Java and C#, I'm always looking around for things to learn to deepen and broaden my skills. That's why I know some Python and Go, played with Android, know a thing or two about security, and recently discovered the wonderful world of Ruby (and Rails). I would love to work with it intensively in my next job.
